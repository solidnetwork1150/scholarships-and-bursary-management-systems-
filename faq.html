❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GSU Scholarship Portal
📝 Who is eligible to apply for scholarships?
All currently enrolled students of Gombe State University are eligible to apply for scholarships and bursaries. Each scholarship has specific eligibility criteria based on academic merit, financial need, department, and level of study. Review the requirements carefully before submitting your application.
💰 How much funding can I receive?
Scholarship amounts range from ₦200,000 to ₦750,000 per academic year depending on the scholarship type. Merit-based scholarships typically offer higher amounts, while bursaries provide essential financial support for tuition and living expenses.
📋 What documents do I need to upload?
You will typically need your academic transcript, a recommendation letter from a faculty member, proof of financial status, a valid student ID, and a personal statement explaining why you deserve the scholarship. Specific requirements vary by scholarship type.
⏰ When are the application deadlines?
Deadlines vary by scholarship. Currently, the STEM Leaders Bursary closes November 30, 2025, Merit Excellence Scholarship closes December 15, 2025, and Community Service Award closes January 10, 2026. Check individual scholarship pages for exact dates.
📊 How is the selection process conducted?
Applications are evaluated based on clear, published criteria — academic merit (40%), financial need (30%), and personal achievements/essay (30%). A committee of faculty members reviews all applications, and shortlisted candidates may be invited for an interview.
💸 When and how will I receive the funds?
Once your application is approved, funds are disbursed directly to your registered bank account or applied to your tuition fees within 14 working days. You will receive email and SMS notifications at every stage of the disbursement process.
🔄 Can I apply for multiple scholarships?
Yes! You can apply for as many scholarships as you qualify for. Your profile information is saved once, making it easy to apply to multiple opportunities without re-entering your details each time.
🔐 Is my personal information secure?
Absolutely. All personal and financial information is encrypted and stored securely. GSU Scholarship Portal uses industry-standard security measures to protect your data. We never share your information with third parties without your consent.
