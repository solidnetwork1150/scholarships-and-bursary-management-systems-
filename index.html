Scholarship & Bursary
Management system

Empowering students of Gombe State University with seamless access to financial aid opportunities. Apply, track, and secure your future — all in one place.

Easy Online Application

Say goodbye to long queues and paperwork. Our intuitive online portal lets you complete your entire scholarship application in minutes. Simply fill in your details, upload the required documents, and submit — no physical forms, no office visits, no hassle. Thousands of GSU students have already applied successfully through this platform, and you can join them today.

Real-Time Status Tracking

Never wonder what is happening with your application again. From the moment you hit submit, you can monitor every stage of your application in real time. Receive instant notifications when your status changes, when a reviewer adds feedback, or when a final decision has been made. Stay informed and plan your academic future with complete confidence.

Transparent Selection

Every application is evaluated based on clear, published criteria — academic merit, financial need, and personal achievements. Our selection process is fully transparent, ensuring equal opportunity for every student of Gombe State University. You deserve to know that your future is being decided fairly, and we make sure of it. No hidden criteria, no favoritism — just honest evaluation.

Secure Fund Disbursement

Once your scholarship or bursary is approved, funds are disbursed directly to your registered bank account within 14 working days. Every transaction is encrypted and tracked for your security. You will receive email and SMS confirmations at every stage — from approval to payment completion — ensuring complete transparency and peace of mind.
